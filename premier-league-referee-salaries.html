Premier League Referee Salaries

Premier League referees earn a base match fee of approximately £1,150 to £1,500 per game. In addition to this, they receive an annual retainer between £38,000 and £42,000, depending on experience and FIFA status.

Assistant referees and fourth officials are paid slightly less, with match fees ranging from £850 to £1,200.

Fun Fact: Top FIFA-certified referees can also officiate international tournaments like the World Cup, earning additional bonuses from UEFA or FIFA.
