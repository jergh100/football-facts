What is a Hat-Trick in Football?

A hat-trick occurs when a player scores three goals in a single match. It's a celebrated feat and often earns the player the match ball as a memento.

A perfect hat-trick is even rarer — it includes one goal scored with the right foot, one with the left foot, and one with the head, all in the same game.

Fun Fact: The term 'hat-trick' originated in cricket in the 1800s, when a player who took three wickets in a row was given a hat as a reward. It was later adopted into football culture.
