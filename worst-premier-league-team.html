Worst Premier League Team

The unfortunate title of the worst Premier League team goes to Derby County, who had a historically poor season in 2007/08.

They finished with just 11 points from 38 games — a record low. Derby won only one match and ended the season with a goal difference of -69.

Did you know? Their only win came against Newcastle United, and they conceded a whopping 89 goals.
