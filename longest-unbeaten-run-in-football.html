Longest Unbeaten Run in Football

The longest unbeaten run in top-flight domestic football is held by AC Milan, who went 58 matches without defeat in Serie A between 1991 and 1993.

In international football, Italy went 37 games unbeaten from 2018 to 2021 — the longest run for any national team.

In the Premier League, Arsenal famously went 49 games unbeaten between 2003 and 2004, earning the nickname “The Invincibles.”

Fun Fact: These runs often span across seasons and competitions — keeping them going is one of football’s biggest challenges!
