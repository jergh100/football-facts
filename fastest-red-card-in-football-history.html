Fastest Red Card in Football History

The fastest red card ever shown in professional football occurred just 2 seconds into the match.

Lee Todd received the red in 2000 while playing for Cross Farm Park Celtic. As soon as the referee blew the whistle to start the game, Todd responded, “F*** me, that was loud,” and was immediately sent off for foul language.

In terms of gameplay: Some players have received red cards within 10–15 seconds of making their first challenge — including tackle-based reds for reckless fouls straight from kick-off.
