What Do Goalkeepers Wear?

Football goalkeepers wear specialized equipment designed to enhance performance and provide protection. Their gear includes:

  • Goalkeeper Gloves: Essential for grip and shock absorption when catching or parrying the ball.
  • Padded Jersey: Often includes elbow or shoulder padding to reduce impact from dives.
  • Goalkeeper Shorts or Trousers: Some wear padded shorts, while others prefer long padded trousers for sliding.
  • Football Boots: Typically firm ground or soft ground, depending on pitch type — just like outfield players.
  • Optional Gear: Knee pads, finger saves, compression wear, or headgear for added protection.

Fun Fact: Goalkeepers must wear a kit that is clearly distinguishable from both teams and the referee — that's why their jerseys are often bright or unusual colors.
