How Long Is a Football Match?

A standard football match is played over 90 minutes, split into two halves of 45 minutes each. There's a 15-minute halftime break between the two halves.

Additional stoppage time (also known as injury time) is added at the end of each half to account for any delays, substitutions, or time-wasting during play.

If the match is part of a knockout competition, it can also include 30 minutes of extra time (two 15-minute halves) and, if still tied, a penalty shootout to determine the winner.

Did you know? The longest official football match lasted over 3 hours, due to multiple extra time periods and shootout delays.
