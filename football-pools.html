Football Pools: Do They Still Exist?

Football pools were once the go-to weekend gamble for millions of Brits. But are they still around — and can you still play them today?

What Are Football Pools?

Football pools are a type of game where players predict the outcomes of football matches — typically focusing on score draws (1–1, 2–2, etc.). Points are awarded for correct predictions, and prizes go to those with the highest scores.

A Bit of History

The football pools date back to 1923 with companies like Littlewoods and Vernons. By the 1980s, over 10 million people were playing each week in the UK.
