Smallest Stadium in the Premier League

The smallest stadium in the Premier League by capacity is Luton Town’s Kenilworth Road.

Kenilworth Road has an official capacity of around 11,500, which is significantly smaller than most other top-flight stadiums that hold 30,000 to 60,000+ fans.

Fun Fact: The stadium famously has an entrance that runs through a row of terraced houses — making it one of the most unique grounds in professional football.
