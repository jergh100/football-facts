How long is a football match?

A football match lasts 90 minutes, split into two halves, with a halftime break and potential added time.

Most goals in a Premier League season

Erling Haaland scored 36 goals in 2022/23 — the most in a single PL season.

Fastest Premier League player

Kyle Walker clocked 37.31 km/h — the league's top sprint speed.

Premier League referee salaries

Referees earn up to £1,500 per match with annual retainers around £42,000.

Worst Premier League team

Derby County got just 11 points in 2007/08 — a record low.

Fastest football player ever

Mbappé, Hakimi and Davies have all hit speeds over 36 km/h.

What is a hat-trick in football?

A hat-trick is when a player scores three goals in one match.

What do goalkeepers wear?

Gloves, padded kits, boots, and sometimes extra protection for dives and collisions.

Brazil national football team players

Includes stars like Vinícius Jr, Rodrygo, Casemiro and Alisson.

List of Premier League winners

All champions since 1992, from Manchester United to Manchester City.

Top 10 Premier League goal scorers

Alan Shearer tops the list with 260 goals, followed by Kane and Rooney.

The Rungrado 1st of May Stadium in North Korea holds over 114,000.

Fastest red card in football history

Who invented football?

The modern game began in England in the 1800s — with official rules set in 1863.

Youngest Premier League player

Ethan Nwaneri debuted for Arsenal aged just 15 years, 181 days.

Smallest stadium in the Premier League

Kenilworth Road (Luton Town) seats just around 11,500.

What does offside mean in football?

Offside prevents attackers from gaining an unfair advantage near the goal.

Most expensive football transfer

Neymar moved to PSG in 2017 for a world-record €222 million.

What is VAR in football?

VAR is the Video Assistant Referee system that helps review major decisions.
