How long is a football match?

A standard football match lasts 90 minutes with added stoppage time and a 15-minute break.

Most goals in a Premier League season

Erling Haaland holds the record with 36 goals in 2022/23.

Fastest Premier League player

Kyle Walker clocked in at 37.31 km/h — the fastest in Premier League history.

Premier League referee salaries

Refs earn about £1,500 per match with annual retainers of up to £42,000.

Worst Premier League team

Derby County finished the 2007/08 season with just 11 points — the lowest ever.

Fastest football player ever

Mbappé, Davies, Hakimi and others have all clocked speeds near or above 36 km/h in elite matches.
