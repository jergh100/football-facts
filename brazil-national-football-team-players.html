Brazil National Football Team Players

The Brazil national team, known as the Seleção, is one of the most iconic sides in world football, with a record five World Cup titles.

Key current players (as of 2024) include:

  • Vinícius Júnior – Real Madrid winger, known for his explosive pace and flair
  • Rodrygo – Another Real Madrid star, lethal in attack
  • Casemiro – Experienced defensive midfielder, currently with Manchester United
  • Marquinhos – PSG defender and captain figure
  • Alisson Becker – Liverpool’s #1 and Brazil's top-choice goalkeeper

Did you know? Brazil is the only country to have appeared in every single FIFA World Cup since 1930.
