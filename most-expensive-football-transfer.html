Most Expensive Football Transfer

The most expensive football transfer in history is Neymar's move from Barcelona to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) in 2017.

PSG activated his release clause, paying a record-breaking fee of €222 million (around £200 million or $263 million at the time).

This move redefined the transfer market, setting a new bar for player valuations and financial muscle in football.

Fun Fact: Neymar’s fee is still unmatched, even years later — although players like Kylian Mbappé and Jude Bellingham have come close!
