Oldest Football Player to Play Professionally

The oldest professional footballer ever is Kazuyoshi Miura, a Japanese forward born in 1967.

As of 2024, he is still under contract and playing at 57 years old, currently with Oliveirense in Portugal (on loan from Yokohama FC).

Nicknamed “King Kazu,” Miura began his career in the 1980s and has outlasted multiple footballing generations.

Fun Fact: He was already a professional footballer before Kylian Mbappé, Jude Bellingham, or Erling Haaland were born.
