Fastest Premier League Player

As of the latest stats, Kyle Walker holds the record for the fastest top speed in a Premier League match, clocking in at 37.31 km/h.

Other notable speedsters include Mykhailo Mudryk, Darwin Núñez, and Adama Traoré, all of whom have recorded speeds above 36 km/h in competitive play.

Fun Fact: Despite being a full-back, Walker consistently outruns forwards during counterattacks — an underrated asset in modern football.
