Top 10 Premier League Goal Scorers

Here are the top all-time scorers in Premier League history (as of 2024):

  • 1. Alan Shearer – 260 goals
  • 2. Harry Kane – 213 goals
  • 3. Wayne Rooney – 208 goals
  • 4. Andy Cole – 187 goals
  • 5. Sergio Agüero – 184 goals
  • 6. Frank Lampard – 177 goals
  • 7. Thierry Henry – 175 goals
  • 8. Robbie Fowler – 163 goals
  • 9. Jermain Defoe – 162 goals
  • 10. Michael Owen – 150 goals

Fun Fact: Alan Shearer scored all his goals for just two clubs — Blackburn Rovers and Newcastle United — and no one has broken his record yet.
