What Is a Clean Sheet in Football?

A clean sheet means a team has finished a match without conceding any goals.

It’s often used to measure the performance of a team’s defense and especially the goalkeeper.

Examples:

  • A 0–0 draw = clean sheet for both sides
  • A 2–0 win = clean sheet for the winning team
  • A 3–1 win = no clean sheet

Fun Fact: Goalkeepers are awarded “clean sheets” in official stats, and many aim to lead the league in clean sheets each season.
