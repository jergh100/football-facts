What Is a Brace in Football?

A brace in football refers to a player scoring two goals in a single match.

The term comes from old English hunting language where “a brace” meant two of the same — like a brace of pheasants.

So when a player scores twice, commentators and fans often say they “scored a brace.”

Fun Fact: Scoring three goals is called a hat-trick, and scoring four is sometimes referred to as a “haul.”
