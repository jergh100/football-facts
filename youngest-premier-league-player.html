Youngest Premier League Player

The youngest player ever to appear in a Premier League match is Ethan Nwaneri of Arsenal.

He made his debut on 18 September 2022 at just 15 years and 181 days old, coming on as a substitute against Brentford.

Before Nwaneri, the record was held by Harvey Elliott (Fulham), who debuted at 16 years and 30 days.

Fun Fact: Ethan Nwaneri is the only player in Premier League history to make an appearance before turning 16.
