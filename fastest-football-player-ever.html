Who is the Fastest Football Player Ever?

Over the years, several footballers have amazed fans with their blazing speed. The top contenders for the title of fastest football player ever include:

  • Antonio Rüdiger – 36.7 km/h (recorded during Euro 2020)
  • Achraf Hakimi – 36.48 km/h (Bundesliga)
  • Alphonso Davies – 36.51 km/h (Champions League)
  • Adama Traoré – consistently over 36 km/h in PL games
  • Kylian Mbappé – up to 38 km/h in open play (widely cited, though unofficial)

Fun Fact: While some of these speeds are unofficial, Mbappé is often considered the fastest forward in elite football due to his in-game sprints during Champions League matches.
