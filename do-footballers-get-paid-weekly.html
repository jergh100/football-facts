Do Footballers Get Paid Weekly?

Yes, most professional footballers — especially in Europe — are paid weekly. Their contracts usually state a “weekly wage” figure, although it’s often paid out monthly for accounting purposes.

Clubs structure deals to include:

  • Base weekly wage
  • Performance bonuses (goals, clean sheets, appearances)
  • Image rights and sponsorships

Top players like those in the Premier League often have wages of £100,000+ per week. Lower league players might earn between £500 and £5,000 weekly depending on the club’s finances.

Fun Fact: Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo have contracts that earn them more in a week than most people make in a year!
