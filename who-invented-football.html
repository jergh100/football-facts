Who Invented Football?

The origins of football trace back thousands of years, but the modern game as we know it today was invented in England in the mid-19th century.

In 1863, the Football Association (FA) was formed in London. It was here that the first standardized set of rules was created — marking the official birth of association football (soccer).

Earlier forms of the game existed in ancient China (called cuju), Greece, Rome, and among native South American cultures. However, the FA’s rulebook is what truly defined football globally.

Fun Fact: Rugby and football were once the same sport — they split after a disagreement over whether handling the ball was allowed!
