Most Goals in a Premier League Season

The current record for most goals scored in a single Premier League season is held by Erling Haaland, who netted 36 goals in the 2022/23 season for Manchester City.

Haaland broke the long-standing joint record of 34 goals set by Andy Cole (1993/94) and Alan Shearer (1994/95) — both of whom scored their tallies when the league had 42 games instead of 38.

Fun Fact: Haaland achieved his record without taking penalties for a large part of the season, making his goal-per-minute ratio even more impressive.
